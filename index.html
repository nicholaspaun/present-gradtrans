Chemlogic

  • Chemlogic is a logic program for balancing chemical equations and converting chemical formulas to and from chemical names, using a database of chemical element and polyatomic group information, a set of grammars, and a linear equation solver. Chemlogic can detect and provide guidance for resolving syntax and other errors and has a user-friendly Web interface.

Chemlogic — Work and Research

  • The original version of Chemlogic was a simple program that I wrote to simplify my chemistry homework.
  • Last year, I decided to explore Computer Science, by writing and studying various programs and algorithms in an Independent Directed Studies course.
  • I decided to extend Chemlogic to balance chemical equations.
  • The project developed far beyond my expectations. I ended up adding many new features, a user-friendly interface and new error-detection and explanation capabilities (pictured).

Chemlogic — the Canada-Wide Science Fair

  • When Mr. Tchir suggested entering the project into the local science fair (which I won), I had to adapt the project with in a short timeframe.
  • My project was nationally recognized at the CWSF. I recieved a Bronze Excellence Award.
  • Going to the science fairs gave me the opportunity to present my project to scientific experts (pictured) and to learn how to write science papers and create conference posters.
